Goals of the coaching program for the Organization of American Statesâ€™ Open Government Fellows:

  • To support the development of the open government projects carried out by the Organization of American States (OAS) Open Government Fellows with frameworks in agile and human centered project development, coaching and access to experts in the field.
  • To provide a space for peer-to-peer learning, feedback and sharing among Fellows that enhances network formation.

Course Description

<p>The purpose of the Fellowship on Open Government in the Americas is to create a network of change agents that bring together government stakeholders from the public sphere, civil society and the private sector for a targeted knowledge transfer within the region and to foster regional cooperation and exchange on Open Government strategies and experiences across the Americas. The first cohort was comprised of 24 fellows from 16 countries.</p> <p>Carried out in Spanish, the Fellowship starts with an initial online training course on open government carried out by OAS and then a one week retreat. This is followed by six months of coaching the teams working on group projects and finishes with another one week retreat.</p> <p>The general pedagogic approach of the coaching program includes a Spanish speaking coach with expertise in governance and civic innovation who will run all the sessions. The curriculum will focus on taking projects from idea to implementation, with high-quality original or curated content from the best expert practitioners. Rigorous attention will be given to problem definition, proven to be one of the keys of success in innovation projects. Sessions will focus on equipping Fellows with the tools to become agents of change, whether or not they implement the specific project of the fellowship, by exploring how to overcome obstacles to implementation. Fellows will learn about human-centered and agile approaches to designing solutions.</p>

Course Format

1 faculty-led session every month for 2 hours (one hour is with a guest expert and one hour is an all-group session); and a personalized schedule of peer-to-peer and one-on-one coaching sessions.

Course Duration: 24 weeks
