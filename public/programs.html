The Programs We Offer

Coaching Programs

Online coaching sessions customized for those who have already started – or are just about to start – a civic tech project focused on a specific theme or issue. Sessions meet every week for 4 weeks or every other week for 8 weeks. Limited to 10 teams or individuals

  • WHO Civic entrepreneurs with an identified project on a specific civic tech theme. Maximum of 10 individuals and/or teams per program. By application only
  • WHAT Intensive, individualized coaching
  • WHEN Every week (for 4 weeks) or every other week (for 8 weeks)
  • WHERE Online

More Information

Workshops

Intensive, online 3 week-long substantive introductions to support teams about to start or already engaged at a specific stage of a civic innovation project.

  • WHO Teams of civic entrepreneurs, especially in government or other organizations. Maximum of 24 individuals and/or 6 teams
  • WHAT Highly interactive workshop, leveraging a combination of mini-lectures, team-based exercises, and post-session follow-up
  • WHEN 1-2 day sessions by arrangement
  • WHERE In-person

Courses and Project Clinics

Masters - and professional-level project-based courses and clinics focused on the innovative application of civic technology to public problems. Online or blended delivery.

  • WHO Graduate students and professionals either for credit or for certification
  • WHAT Semester-long, project and/or case-based immersion in the all the stages of designing and implementing a civic tech project; structured access to support from subject matter experts, tech experts, mentors, coaches, and peers
  • WHEN Weekly for 5, 12 or 14 weeks
  • WHERE Blended (in person and online)
